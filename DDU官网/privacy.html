PERSONAL INFORMATION–MANAGE THE REQUESTS RELATING TO THE WEBSITE
The contact form provided on the site, droidigitaluniverse.com is a personal information form managed by Droi Digital Universe.
Legal basis: Article 6 (1) a of the General Data Protection Regulation – GDPR
Purpose of information collecting
The aim is to manage the requests addressed within the contact form of the droidigitaluniverse.com website.
It allows Droi Digital Universe to: receive requests within the contact forms addressed to the business administrator and further follow-up the requests.
Data processed
Classifications of information handled:
1. Identity (name, first name, company) ;
2. Email address ;
3. Messages send to Droi Digital Universe
Data source: The data is directly provided by the user of the site droidigitaluniverse.com
Mandatory nature of data collection: The mandatory collection of your relevant information (company, email, etc.) in the form on the droidigitaluniverse.com official website is to respond to your request more accurately.
Automated decision making: The website does not provide for automated decision making.
People concerned
The information handling concerns individuals who wish to resolve an inquiry on the
droidigitaluniverse.com site.
Recipients of the data
Categories of recipients
The recipients of the data are:
1. the communications department of Droi Digital Universe
2. where applicable, the other services of Droi Digital Universe and its subsidiaries
Data transfers outside the EU: Data concerning an application on the initiative of a subsidiary located outside the European Union may be transferred outside the European Union.
Data retention period
The data is stored for 3 years from the moment of sending the message.
Your rights with respect to data about you
You can access and obtain a copy of the data concerning you, object to its processing, rectify or delete it. You also have the right to restrict the processing of your data.
Droi Digital Universe's Data Protection Officer (DPO) is your point of contact for any requests to exercise your rights.
Contact the DPO via email: ddu@droidigitaluniverse.com
