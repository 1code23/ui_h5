Q1.How to clone the applications？

a) You can click the icon like on Lower right corner of main interface and the page jumps to the application lists, and then you can choose the application to clone it.

b) Click the cloned application icon to log in with another account.

Q2.How to uninstall the cloned apps?

you can click the menu icon like on top right corner of app icon,and then click the menu named "uninstall app".

Q3.What happens after the Multi Lab is uninstalled?

If you uninstalled Multi Lab, your cloned apps will also be uninstalled.

Q4.Why Multi Lab applied for many permissions?

Multi Lab provides a virtual environment. The permission application is to ensure that the functions of the dual-open application operate normally. We will not violate your privacy and security.

Q5.Will the second account affect the function of the original account?

it does not influence.The second account is running in a completely different environment, and each account running at the same time does not affect each other.

Q6.Why the cloned apps can't receive the message notification?

a) You must open the notification permission of Multi Lab.

b) You must ensure that Multi Lab obtains the system's networking authority.
